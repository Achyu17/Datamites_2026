# 📊 Sales Effectiveness Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview
This project builds a Machine Learning model to predict the **quality of sales leads** for FicZon Inc., an IT solutions company.

The goal is to automatically classify leads into **High Potential** or **Low Potential** so that the sales team can focus on the most valuable leads and improve overall sales performance.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
FicZon Inc generates most of its leads through digital channels and its website. Currently, lead categorization is done manually by the sales team, which can lead to inconsistent decisions.

Using Machine Learning, we aim to build a model that predicts **lead quality automatically**, helping the company improve sales effectiveness.

---

## 📊 Dataset Features

The dataset contains information about sales leads including:

- Created – Lead creation date
- Product_ID – Product identifier
- Source – Source of the lead
- Mobile – Customer phone number
- EMAIL – Customer email
- Sales_Agent – Assigned sales representative
- Location – Customer location
- Delivery_Mode – Delivery method
- Status – Lead quality (Target Variable)

Target Variable:
Status
